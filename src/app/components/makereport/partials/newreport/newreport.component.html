About Reporting

Welcome to the Infrastructure Reporting Service. If you see a problem, please report it here to enable our dedicated workforce to respond. Selecting the type of problem will assist in directing the report more quickly to the correct section or Team who can deal with the problem. You have the option of using a map to pinpoint the location of the problem and also the option to upload a picture to help us identify and prioritise the problem. Please review your report before submitting.
